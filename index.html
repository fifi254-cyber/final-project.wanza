FAQs

What is included in a typical safari package?

All accommodations, meals, game drives, and transfers are included. Optional excursions may be extra.

Is it safe to go on safari?

Absolutely. Our tours are led by professional guides who prioritize your safety at all times.

What should I pack?

Light clothing, sunscreen, insect repellent, binoculars, and a camera are recommended.

Can I customize my safari experience?

Yes! We offer custom packages to suit your interests, schedule, and group size.

Are safaris suitable for children?

Yes, many of our tours are family-friendly and include special activities for kids.
